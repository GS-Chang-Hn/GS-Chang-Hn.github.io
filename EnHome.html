About Me

I am currently a Ph.D. candidate in the School of Computer Science at Hefei University of Technology, under the supervision of Prof. Kui Yu. I obtained my master's degree in Computer Science and Technology from School of Computer Science, Hefei University of Technology in June 2021. Prior to that, I received my bachelor's degree in Computer Science and Technology from Anhui Normal University in June 2018.

My research interests mainly include causal feature selection, causal structure learning, federated causal discovery, etc.
