About me

I am currently pursuing a Ph.D. degree in the School of Computer Science at Hefei University of Technology, under the supervision of Professor Kui Yu.

I obtained my Master's degree from the School of Computer and Information at Hefei University of Technology in June 2021. Prior to that, I received my Bachelor's degree in Computer Science and Technology from the School of Computer Science and Information at Anhui Normal University in June 2018.

Research Interests: causal feature selection, causal structure learning, federated causal discovery.
